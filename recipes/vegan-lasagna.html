Vegan Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cups spinach
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup vegan ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Instructions

    1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
    3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, and sauté until translucent.
    4. Add zucchini, bell pepper, and mushrooms. Cook until vegetables are tender.
    5. Add spinach and cook until wilted. Season with salt and pepper.
    6. In a baking dish, spread a layer of marinara sauce. Place 3 lasagna noodles on top.
    7. Spread half of the vegetable mixture over the noodles, followed by half of the vegan ricotta cheese.
    8. Repeat layers, ending with marinara sauce on top.
    9. Sprinkle nutritional yeast over the top layer.
    10. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes.
    11. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
